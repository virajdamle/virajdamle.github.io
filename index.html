What I do

I am a Doctoral degree student in Mechanical Engineering department at Arizona State University. My graduate research has a blend of Surface Chemistry, Microscopy and Thermal Engineering.

Research Interest

My ‘current’ research interests include, but not limited to, Surface patterning, surface wettability characterization and doing experimental research. Additionally I am also interested in Refrigeration, Heat Transfer, Fluid Mechanics.

Personal

I belong to Pune, India which is in Western India about 100 miles (180km) east of Mumbai. City dates back its significant existence to 16th century when it was the capital of Maratha Dynasty. However now city is growing fast and it is one of the prominent Information-Technology hubs in the country. While coming from a small working-class family, I did my primary schooling in Pune in my mother tounge ‘Marathi’. I believe this forms a foundation of my academic career and myself as person. My family and friends are my prominent sources of inspiration.
